SAVANNAH KUNOVSKY

EMERGING TECH PRODUCT & DESIGN LEADERSHIP

I’m a visionary leader who builds the next generation of products. As Managing Director, I run IDEO’s Emerging Technology Lab, leading the teams that make breakthrough products and strategic futures for a global network of clients like Meta, Google, Microsoft, and Sony. My previous work includes pioneering emotion AI for mental health with Dr. John Gottman and co-founding Moringa School, a chain of tech education schools in Africa. I started off as a software engineer, building products.

I’ve spoken at events like SxSW, Fast Company Innovation Festival, the MIT Media Lab, and Harvard. I’ve delivered international keynotes, created interactive emerging technology museums, and published numerous articles.

My work has been recognized by MIT, Forbes, and the World Economic Forum. I’m an early stage advisor and investor who has propelled startups from ideation through Series C, and I sit on the board of Duke’s Design & Technology Innovation Master’s program.
